Cute (Remix)

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  141 words  |  Tandra Barner

The Big Baby spits grown man business on the "Cute" remix, teaming up with Cardi B for an alternate take on his endearing hit. The beat remains the same, but Cardi flips the narrative with a scathing verse, starting things off with "you think I'm cute, but I think you ugly." The juxtaposition between D.R.A.M's innocent, childlike persona and Cardi's bad bitch demeanor gives "Cute" a new life, especially when Cardi continues the upward climb to stardom with a dope verse.

If you like an original, you should check out this remix. It's a little more aggressive, but still fits within the song's main theme.
