The Great Buckeye Lament: How Many Times Has Ryan Day Lost to Michigan?

Ah, The Game. That glorious, tension-filled, scarlet-and-gray-versus-maize-and-blue showdown that strikes fear (or perhaps glee, depending on your fandom) into the hearts of college football fans everywhere. But for Ohio State Buckeye fans under the reign of Head Coach Ryan Day, there's been a nagging question swirling around the horseshoe like a rogue Brutus Buckeye mascot: just how many times has Day tasted defeat at the hands of the hated Wolverines?

Buckeye Bliss and a Bumpy Start

Day's tenure began with a bang, folks! In his rookie year as head coach (2019), he steered the Buckeyes to a resounding 56-27 victory over Michigan, proving he could lead the scarlet tide to victory. Dreams of a new era of dominance danced in the eyes of Buckeye Nation.

The Plot Thickens...Uh Oh

But then, things took a turn. 2021 saw the Wolverines pull off a 41-27 upset in Ann Arbor, shattering those dreams like a dropped interception. Buckeye Nation held its breath. Was this a blip, or a sign of things to come?

The Streak is Alive (and Not in a Good Way)

2022 arrived, and with it, another Michigan victory, this time 30-24. The Wolverines had now claimed two wins in a row, a feat not seen since the dark ages of 1999 (we won't mention the even darker times of the 1990s...). Buckeye fans started to sweat. Was Ryan Day losing his touch? Had Jim Harbaugh, the khaki-clad nemesis, finally figured out the scarlet and gray?

So, What's the Damage, Doc?

Alright, alright, I'll cut to the chase. As of today, June 4th, 2024, Ryan Day has faced the Wolverines four times, emerging victorious just once. That means he's suffered a gut-wrenching three losses to the maize and blue.

Buckeye Fans: Don't Panic (Yet)

Now, before you start prepping the "Fire Day" signs, take a deep breath. Three losses might sting, but Day's overall record is still stellar. He's a championship-caliber coach, and there's plenty of time to turn things around.

How to FAQs:

How to cope with a Michigan loss?

  • Distraction is key! Binge-watch your favorite show, bury yourself in a good book, or take up falconry (hey, it worked for Hawkeye Pierce in MASH).
  • Seek solace in fellow Buckeye fans. Misery loves company, as they say.
  • Remember, there's always next year! The cycle of The Game continues, and revenge is a dish best served cold...or scarlet-hot.

How to avoid Michigan fans after a loss?

  • Invisibility cloak (optional, but highly recommended).
  • Headphones blasting your favorite Buckeye fight song.
  • Pretend you have a sudden, uncontrollable case of amnesia. ("Who are you? Where am I? What's a football?")

How to prepare for the next Ohio State-Michigan game?

  • Stock up on scarlet and gray apparel. You wanna be LOUD and PROUD.
  • Practice your cheers. Those lungs gotta be in peak performance.
  • Mentally prepare for a rollercoaster of emotions. It's The Game, baby! Buckle up.

How to (hopefully) celebrate a Buckeye victory over Michigan?

  • Let loose! Jump for joy, scream your head off, do the Buckeye shuffle. You earned it.
  • Gloat responsibly. Don't rub it in too hard (remember, what goes around comes around).
  • Enjoy the moment. A win over Michigan is a beautiful thing. Savor it.
