Nigerians have taken to singer Teni's official Instagram page to wish her well on her birthday.

The Billionaire crooner turned a new age on Wednesday, December 23, and she shared the news with her fans and followers.

As expected, the musician shared beautiful photos to mark her new age and she surprised her followers by wearing a bone straight wig.

Many are familiar with the singer's tomboyish look, so seeing the artiste wear a wig was a pleasant surprise.

In one of the photos, Teni was donned in a long black shirt dress with black trousers and pink sneakers to match.

Teni donned a black bone straight wig and she held it in her hands as she posed for the camera.

In the second photo, the singer wore a high-low pink top with black trousers. Her hair fell on her shoulder and one hand was on her waist as she posed for the camera with a smile.
