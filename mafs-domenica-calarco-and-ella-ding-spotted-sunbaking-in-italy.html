They didn’t find love on Married At First Sight – but Domenica Calarco and Ella Ding found each other and are now closer than ever.

The MAFS besties are currently enjoying a holiday on the Amalfi Coast, Italy, where the reality show brides have been snapped sunbaking topless on a boat.

Dom opted for a turquoise blue G-string bikini to enjoy their day out on the water, while Ella went for a similar black number that showed off her tattoos.

At one stage, the sun-safe pair – who also host the Sit With Us podcast together – rubbed sunscreen onto each others backs.

Dom was also snapped jumping off the boat and into the clear blue water.

Both the MAFS contestants are in Italy after a trip to LA to attend a glitzy launch party for Australian brand Booby Tape.

The star-studded event launched a new body bronzer in collaboration with Kylie Jenner’s best friend Stassie Karanikolaou.

Other famous attendees included mega influencers Dixie & Charli D’Amelio, Travis Barker’s son Landon and Brazilian model Alessandra Ambrosio.

The Aussie influencers got dressed up to rub shoulders with the famous stars, with Ella wearing a custom Ogliaoro Couture gown while Dom went for an all leather look.
