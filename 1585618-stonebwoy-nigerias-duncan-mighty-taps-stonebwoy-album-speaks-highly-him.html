Duncan Mighty Enlists Stonebwoy for His New Album, Speaks Highly Of Him

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  429 words  |  Kelle Repass
  • Nigerian star Duncan Mighty has arrived in Ghana to kickstart the promotion of his upcoming album, which features Stonebwoy
  • In an interview, the Nigerian crooner shared the backstory of his much-loved collaboration with Stonebwoy
  • A video of the artists together has caught the attention of many fans, who have begun counting down to the song's release

Nigerian singer Duncan Mighty has disclosed in a recent interview that Stonebwoy will be featured in his upcoming album.

Speaking to 3MusicTV's Culture Daily team, Duncan Mighty spoke highly of Stonebwoy and established that their upcoming project was a great fusion of sounds from Nigeria and Ghana.

The upcoming collaboration has got scores of fans excited as they look forward to the song's release.
