How many kids does Lil Wayne have?

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  375 words  |  Billy Koelling

RAPPER Lil Wayne has four children with four different women.

A cryptic tweet from the musician on April 20, 2021, has fans thinking he is married again.

How many kids does Lil Wayne have?

Lil Wayne, who is 38, has four children: Reginae Carter, Dwayne Michael Carter III, Cameron Carter, and Neal Carter.

Actress Reginae Carter, who is 22, is the daughter of Lil Wayne and TV personality Toya Johnson.

Lil Wayne was just 16 when Reginae was born. He later was married to Toya from 2004 to 2006.

Dwayne Michael Carter III, who is 12, is Lil Wayne's son with a woman named Sarah Vivan, according to LilWayneHQ.com.

Actress Lauren London gave birth to Lil Wayne's third child, Cameron Carter, who is 11.

Lil Wayne had his youngest child Neal Carter with singer Nivea B. Hamilton. Neal is also 11 and was born a few months after Cameron.

Is Lil Wayne getting married again?

Fans believe that Lil Wayne is now married to girlfriend Denise Bidot.

"Happiest man alive! Today is the beginning of our forever. Forever?? Forever, ever?? FOREVER EVER!!!!! The Carters," he wrote on Twitter on April 20.

Fans replied to the tweet to send him congratulations.

One fan asked "who tf you getting married to?" as a following person responded: "He has a fiancé already lol."
