FAQs

Does New Balance have free shipping?

New Balance offers free shipping on orders of $75 or more. Any orders that are less than this will cost $9.95 for standard shipping. If your order is less than $75, always check this page first for the occasional New Balance free shipping code. There are also 1 and 2 day shipping options available, for an additional cost.  

How do I sign up for New Balance rewards?

You can sign up for the New Balance newsletter to get 10% off promo code on your first order. You’ll also automatically be signed up for the New Balance loyalty program. To sign up, simply visit the New Balance website footer and type your email address. Email sign-up is free and you can opt-out at any time.  

Can I get a student discount at New Balance?

Yes. Students save up to 25% off their orders from New Balance. If you’re shopping for a new look for class, verify your student status to get your New Balances for less. Once you’ve verified your student status, you’ll be sent your unique code. 

Does New Balance have military discount?

Yes. Military personnel or veterans can get 15% off their orders at New Balance. You will need to confirm your work status via ID.Me to get this New Balance coupon.  

What is the return policy at New Balance?

If you’re not 100% happy with your purchase, you can return it to New Balance within 45 days of receipt. Your items should be returned like new with any labels still attached and original shoeboxes included. Go to the New Balance website to start your return and print your free shipping label.
