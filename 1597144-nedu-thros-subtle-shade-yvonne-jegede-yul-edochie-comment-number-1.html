Polygamy Saga: Nedu Reacts to Yvonne Jegede's Apology to May Edochie, Says &quot;Stand by Your Words&quot;

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  408 words  |  Tandra Barner
  • The Honest Bunch podcast host, Chinedu Ani Emmanuel, better known as Nedu, has reacted to his guest, Yvonne Jegede's apology
  • Recall that Yvonne was a guest on an episode of the show, and one of the topics discussed was polygamy
  • The actress had remarked that she had seen nothing wrong in actor Yul Edochie's approach to polygamy

OAP and host of the famous The Honest Bunch podcast, Nedu, has taken a subtle jab at one of his recent guests, Nigerian actress Yvonne Jegede.

Yvonne unintentionally set herself up for drags after she issued a statement concerning May Edochie and her ex-husband, Yul's marital crisis, and she has been taking the heat.

Yvonne gave a detailed account of what she endured during her marriage to Olakunle Fawole, her ex-husband.

Part of the things she said was that she would choose money over love in her next relationship. In one of her statements, she lambasted ladies online who have dragged Yul Edochie for delving into polygamy and said that she saw nothing wrong in what he had done.

After being dragged for her comment, she apologised to May Edochie for her insensitivity and failure to articulate her words appropriately.

Nonetheless, the mother-of-one still keeps getting slammed and suffering severe backlash.

Did Nedu just jab at Yvonne Jegede?

Nedu, the podcast show host, joined to throw a subtle shade at her. Taking to his IG story, the media personality trolled the actress for not standing by her own words.

Nedu wrote:

"If you talk, use your chest stand by your words."

Angela Okorie criticises Yvonne Jegede

Angela Okorie criticised her fellow actress, Yvonne Jegede, heavily for her statement while speaking on Nedu's podcast.
