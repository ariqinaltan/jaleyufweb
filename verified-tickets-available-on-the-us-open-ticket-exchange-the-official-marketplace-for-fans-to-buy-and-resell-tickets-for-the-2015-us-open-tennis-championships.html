Verified Tickets Available on the US Open Ticket Exchange, The Official Marketplace for Fans to Buy

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  147 words  |  Billy Koelling

Ticketmaster is pleased to announce that it is working with the United States Tennis Association (USTA) to bring convenience and security to tennis fans who are purchasing tickets for the 2015 US Open Tennis Championships this summer. 

Fans will be able to pick their perfect seat, get a Ticketmaster Verified ticket and get into the stadium easily, when purchasing tickets through the US Open Ticket Exchange, Ticketmaster.com or through the Ticketmaster mobile app.

Convenient options like mobile delivery, print-at-home, will call and onsite self-service Ticketmaster Kiosks will enable fans to select how they prefer to receive their securely purchased tickets.

Running from Aug. 31 through Sept. 13, the 2015 US Open will be held at the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center in Flushing Meadows, New York.

The US Open Ticket Exchange originally launched in 2008.
