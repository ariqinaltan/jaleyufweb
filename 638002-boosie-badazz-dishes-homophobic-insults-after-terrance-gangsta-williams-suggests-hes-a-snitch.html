Boosie Badazz Dishes Homophobic Insults After Terrance &quot;Gangsta&quot; Williams Suggests He's A Snitch

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  368 words  |  Billy Koelling

Boosie Badazz made two enemies when he called out T.I. over the video clip surrounding his late cousin. Terrance “Gangsta” Williams, Birdman’s half-brother, put Boosie on blast for referring to T.I. as a rat for his supposed hypothetical story about pinning a gun charge on his dead cousin. Ultimately, this led to a back-and-forth between Boosie, T.I., and Williams, who recently dug up some footage of Boosie to paint him as a hypocrite.

Williams shared a video of Boosie discussing charges against the Biloxi police, who he believed robbed him of $1M worth of jewelry. “We’re getting all our private investigators to go down there, build our case. It’s a criminal case and a civil case,” Boosie said in the clip. “This is only the beginning.” For someone who frequently disavows snitches, Terrance Williams questioned whether his stance on cooperating with authorities stands in this instance.

Boosie Badazz Vs. Terrance “Gangsta” Williams

Shortly after T.I. called out Boosie for failing to show up to verify his paperwork, Terrance Williams asked whether Boosie’s criminal case towards the police counted as snitching. “I understand the civil case , but do real street Steppas get a pass when they give statements on criminal cases? Is this the only time they can get on the stand and it will be okay sending someone to jail?” he captioned the post. It didn’t take long for fans to point out the difference between Boosie’s case against the police and those involved in the streets cooperating with authorities.

Boosie didn’t hesitate to chime in on the matter, dishing out homophobic insults to Williams to strip him of his street credibility. The Louisiana rapper shared an old photo of Terrance Williams sitting on the lap of a man. “You a ponk n a certified rat,” he wrote. “You was n Yazoo getting f*cked n f*cking n***as… Boy you a bitch.” Afterward, he stated that the city of New Orleans should denounce Williams entirely. “New Orleans should band this dude!! This is the worst kinda n***a!! He a rat, a family snake n homosexual playin’ gangster,” Boosie wrote. At this point, Williams has yet to respond but we’ll keep you posted if he does.
