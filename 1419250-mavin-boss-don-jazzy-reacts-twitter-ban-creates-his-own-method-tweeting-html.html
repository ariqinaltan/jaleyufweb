Michael Collins Ajereh better known as Don Jazzy appears to have adjusted pretty well following the federal government's decision to ban the use of Twitter in Nigeria.

In a recent Instagram post, the Mavin Record boss made a writeup which he described as his own new method of tweeting.

Commending Bovi for his latest movie, Don Jazzy's post carried the title 'tweet' alongside a bird head emoji.

The benevolent Jazzy

Only a few days ago, Don Jazzy once again went on another round of supporting young people's businesses as a way to make them grow.

On Saturday, May I, a young Nigerian lady with the Twitter handle @BasharKhairat took to the platform to advertise her tie and dye clothes.

Many weeks after - by a dint of luck - Jazzy saw the tweet and said he wants four pieces. In addition, he asked the lady to randomly select 10 of her followers and make one each for them on his bill.
