Johnny Morris’ Early Life and Career

Johnny Morris was born in Springfield, Missouri, and developed a passion for fishing and hunting at a young age. His love for the outdoors led him to start a small fishing section in his father’s liquor store in 1971, which eventually grew into Bass Pro Shops. Today, Bass Pro Shops has over 200 stores across North America and is a leading retailer of outdoor gear.

Establishment of Bass Pro Shops

Johnny Morris’ journey to wealth began with the establishment of Bass Pro Shops. He started by selling fishing tackle in the back of his father’s liquor store. His business grew rapidly, and by 1974, he had opened his first pro shop in Springfield, Missouri. The success of Bass Pro Shops can be attributed to Morris’ understanding of his customers’ needs and his commitment to providing high-quality products.

Expansion and Growth of Bass Pro Shops

Over the years, Bass Pro Shops has expanded significantly, both in terms of physical stores and product offerings. The company now sells a wide range of outdoor gear, including hunting, camping, and boating equipment. This expansion has significantly contributed to Johnny Morris’ net worth.

Acquisition of Cabela’s

In 2017, Johnny Morris made a significant move that further increased his wealth by acquiring Cabela’s, a direct competitor to Bass Pro Shops. The $5.5 billion deal not only expanded Bass Pro Shops’ reach but also consolidated its position in the outdoor retail market.

Investments in Real Estate
