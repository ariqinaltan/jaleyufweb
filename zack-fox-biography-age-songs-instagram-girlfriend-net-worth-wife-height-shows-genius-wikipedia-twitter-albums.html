Biography

Zachary Channing Fox (born December 6, 1990), addressed professionally as Zack Fox, is a famous American stand-up comedian, actor, and rapper. He is additionally a writer and social media sensation.

His hit song Fafo helped him gain popularity. He can already point to significant partnerships with artists like Flying Lotus, Thundercat, and Kenny Beats.

Career

While tweeting jokes in 2013, Zack Fox launched his profession and amassed thousands of followers. He became interested in music and joined the group Awful Records. 

In Flying Lotus‘ 2017 horror comedy Kuso, he both appeared in and penned the screenplay.

In October 2018, he released Square Up, his debut song, in cooperation with producer Kenny Beats. 

After Zack Fox was invited to freestyle at Kenny Beats‘ studio as part of the YouTube series The Cave, it was a very different story.

The song Jesus Is the One (I Got Depression), which both Kenny and Zack Fox performed in the episode, peaked at number one on Spotify’s United States viral chart on July 8, 2019. The song’s success led them to produce an official version even though they had no intention of making it commercially available. 

Additionally, Zack Fox delivered his shut the fuck up talking to me debut studio album, which was published on October 15, 2021. In addition, he had recorded singles like Marinate and Fafo.
