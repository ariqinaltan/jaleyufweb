&quot;You Came Prepared&quot;: Actress Deby Oscar Slays in 5 Gorgeous Attire for Trad Wedding, Fans Gush

 Posted on August 11, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  441 words  |  Kelle Repass
  • Actress Deby Oscar began her journey to holy matrimony in five classy traditional outfits that made her fans gush
  • The beautiful lady did not leave any stone unturned as she slayed in different colours for her special day
  • She adorned expensive accessories that did justice to her outfit, giving her fans some style inspiration

Influencer and content creator Deby Oscar showed excitement as she wore five gorgeous outfits for her traditional wedding to her husband, Uche Hector.

On her Instagram page @debyoscar, she displayed the different styles and had her fans blushing.

For her first look, she wore a fluffy gold attire that flowed to the ground. She complemented her dress with coral beads on her head, neck, wrists, and ears.

She rocked an Igbo-themed corset dress for her second look. Her coral beads were dominant and made her look like royalty.

For her third look, she slayed in a glamorous dress which flowed to the ground and gave her a breathtaking look.

On his part, her husband wore an Igbo-themed outfit, a red cap, and black shoes, giving him a dapper look.

The bride wore a stunning blue dress for her fourth look without the coral beads this time. Her silver earrings and gold bracelets complemented her attire, and her makeup was also on point.

Her stylish orange dress for her fifth look turned heads as they hailed her for wearing the best outfits she could lay her hands on.

She combined the attire with a stylish hand fan and a 'gele'. Her curves were on display as she walked in different angles for her pictures.
