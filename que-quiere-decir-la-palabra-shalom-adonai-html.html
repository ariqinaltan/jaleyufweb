‘Shalom Adonai’ is an expression in the Hebrew language that literally translates as “God’s peace.” It is made up of two words: » Shalom », which means “peace,” and » Adonai », which means “my Lord,” respectively. The word Shalom is used both as a greeting and as a despedida by the Jewish people. It’s similar to our greetings of hola, buenos das, goodbye, and so on.
