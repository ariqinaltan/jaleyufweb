Jason Anthoney Richardson (born January 20, 1981) is a professional basketball player in the United States who now represents the Philadelphia 76ers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Richardson, a 6’6″ 225-pound shooting guard/small forward, was selected fifth overall in the 2001 NBA Draft by the Golden State Warriors from Michigan State, where he won the NBA Rookie Challenge MVP and was named to the All-Rookie Team First Team in his rookie season. He is recognised as one of the NBA’s most flamboyant dunkers, having won the NBA Slam Dunk Contest back-to-back in 2002 and 2003, becoming just the second player after Michael Jordan to do it.
