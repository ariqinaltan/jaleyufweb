Molly McGrath’s career in sports journalism

Before graduating from Boston College, the sportscaster worked as an intern at New England Sports Network and NBC Sports. In 2011, she worked for ESPN.

Still, in 2011, she worked for Boston Celtics as an in-arena host and web reporter.

In January 2012, she was made the host of Celtics Now for Comcast SportsNet New England.

In the summer of the year 2013, she became an update anchor at Fox Sports 1, Los Angeles. In the year 2014, she was promoted to co-host America’s Pregame. Still, in 2014, she worked as the lead sideline reporter for Fox College Football and Fox College Basketball and the sideline reporter for Fox NFL.

In July 2016, she started working for ESPN as a host and sideline reporter. Currently, the vivacious sportscaster is ESPN’s sideline reporter for college football and basketball.
