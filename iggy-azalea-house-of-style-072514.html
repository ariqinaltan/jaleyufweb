- Iggy Azalea has some big shoes to fill—the rapper is bringing House of Style back to MTV, following in the footsteps of supermodels like Naomi Campbell, Cindy Crawford, Linda Evangelista, Helena Christensen and Christy Turlington—the original hosts of the '90s cult hit—and Karlie Kloss and Joan Smalls, who revived the show for an online-only season in 2012. Grazia

- Andrej Pejic reveals she underwent sex reassignment surgery earlier this year and is changing her name to Andreja. "I want to share my story with the world because I think I have a social responsibility," she tells People. "I hope that by being open about this, it becomes less of an issue." The model said that she enjoyed a career that allowed her to model men's and women's clothing, but it's more important that she feels comfortable in her own body. "I have to be true to myself and the career is just going to have to fit around that," she said. People

- Lily Aldridge reveals how she got in shape in time to walk the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show just four months after giving birth to daughter Dixie Pearl: Ballet Beautiful workouts with Mary Helen Bowers. E!
