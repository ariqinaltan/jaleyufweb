FAQs

Does GAP have free shipping?

Yes. You can get free standard shipping on orders of $50 or more when you’ve signed up to Gap Good Rewards. With the Icon tier, you will also get free express shipping on purchases of $50 or over.  

How can I get a GAP email coupon?

By signing up for the GAP newsletter, you can get 15% off your first order at GAP. Simply go to the GAP website footer and type your email address to sign up. Once you have, you’ll also get sales notifications, brand news, and new clothing line updates.  

Does GAP have a text sign-up discount?

You can sign up for GAP text promotion updates although this does not have sign-up discounts. However, you can sign up for the GAP newsletter to get 15% off or use one of our GAP promo codes. Currently, you can get 20% off GAP coupon on your purchase. 

Does GAP have a Black Friday sale?

Yes, shop the GAP Black Friday sales each November for smart savings on casualwear and trending denim. We’ve previously seen up to 50% off selected clothing with these sales, and extra discounts on already discounted items. If you can’t wait till Black Friday, there’s always the dedicated ‘sale’ section of the GAP website. 

What is the return policy at GAP?

If you would like to return your order, you can do so for free within 30 days of receipt. Your items should be returned unworn, unwashed, and without any damage. It is usually advised to leave any tags still attached. Any final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.  

Hints and tips

Sign up for emails: Looking for a 15%-off discount? When you sign up for the GAP newsletter, the brand will send you a coupon code on your first order. To sign up, go to the GAP website footer and type your email address into the box. You’ll also be kept updated on the latest fashion, promotions, and sales. 

Good GAP Rewards: With Good GAP Rewards, you can earn points on each GAP purchase. You’ll also get exclusive discounts and free shipping on orders of over $50. If you would like to sign up, go to the ‘Good GAP Rewards’ page from the footer on the GAP website. You can sign up for free with your email address. 

Save with GapCash: GAP has a few GapCash earning times throughout the year. These are times when you can earn coupons on your purchases to use towards future GAP orders. Our savings experts have noted there is usually a one-week redemption period for these coupons. You can earn $40 - $120 GapCash coupons depending on your order total. When you make a qualifying online purchase of $50 or over, GAP will send your coupon via email. 

GAP Credit Card: If you shop regularly at GAP, you might consider signing up for a GAP credit card. You can get extra Good GAP Rewards points on your purchases and early sales updates. With the GAP Credit Card, you can also earn points at Gap Inc.’s other brands.
