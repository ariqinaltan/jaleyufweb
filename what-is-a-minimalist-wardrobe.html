What is a Minimalist Wardrobe. A minimalist wardrobe contains only what you need and use, is mindful of the impacts of fashion and has an overall goal of making you feel less chaotic about your life.

What is the minimalist style clothing?

Minimalist fashion means having a minimal amount of clothes in your wardrobe that feel right for you and bring joy. Minimalism is the antithesis of the modern consumerist narrative—and for good reason.

How many outfits does a minimalist have?

While most minimalist wardrobes have between 15 and 50 clothing pieces, there's no strict number of items to have in your closet. The key aspect is to have only clothes you need and love and discard the ones you don't use nor need. A minimalist wardrobe is having only the clothes you need and love.
